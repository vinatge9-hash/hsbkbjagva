About CineScope

CineScope is a modern, responsive movie review site built for film lovers and critics alike. Our goal is to provide an elegant, fast interface to browse critically acclaimed titles, discover hidden gems, and share your thoughts with a community that values thoughtful critique.

What we offer

  • • Curated reviews and ratings for popular and indie films.
  • • Lightweight, privacy-friendly local review storage in your browser.
  • • Fast search, filters, and sorting so you find what matters.
  • • Clean, cinematic design that adapts to any device.

Community

Share your reviews locally and explore others' perspectives. We don't require sign-ups.

Design

A minimal, responsive layout built with Tailwind CSS for speed and flexibility.

Privacy

All user-submitted reviews are stored in your browser's local storage — private to you unless you choose to share.
